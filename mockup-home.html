Our story
"We wanted to bring the warmth of a Seoul home kitchen to Ottawa — one bowl at a time."

In's Kitchen was built on one belief: that authentic Korean food, made with love and the finest halal ingredients, can bring people together. We started small on Templeton Street, serving the uOttawa community — and we haven't looked back.

Every dish is rooted in tradition — the kind passed through generations in Korean kitchens. We make it here, in Ottawa, for you.
