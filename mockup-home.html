Our Story
"We wanted to bring the warmth of a Seoul home kitchen to Ottawa — one bowl at a time."

In's Kitchen was born from a simple belief: that authentic Korean food, made with love and the finest halal ingredients, could bring people together. We started small on Templeton Street, serving the uOttawa community, and we haven't looked back since.

Every dish is rooted in tradition — the kind passed down through generations in Korean kitchens. We just happen to make it here, in Ottawa, for you.
