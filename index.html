Our story

Your passport to
Korean flavours

Welcome to In's Kitchen — your passport to the rich and tantalizing flavours of Korea, nestled in the heart of Canada's capital, Ottawa. Our story began with a deep-seated passion for Korean cuisine and a mission to share its authentic, soul-warming dishes with the diverse community of Ottawa, including the vibrant students from the nearby University of Ottawa.

At In's Kitchen, we believe that the key to truly delicious Korean food is the use of high-quality ingredients. That's why we take pride in sourcing only Triple A Beef and 100% halal ingredients, ensuring that every dish we create is not only bursting with flavour but also respects the dietary preferences of our valued customers. Join us on this culinary journey, where tradition meets innovation, and let us transport your taste buds to the vibrant streets of Seoul right here in Ottawa.
