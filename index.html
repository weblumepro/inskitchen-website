Our

A kitchen built on
family recipes

In's Kitchen started with one woman's love for the flavours she grew up with — her mother's sundubu, her grandmother's kimchi, the bulgogi that marked every celebration.

In 2019, she brought those recipes to Ottawa's Lowertown. Everything is made in-house, every morning. The banchan changes with the season. The beef is always Triple A, always halal.
